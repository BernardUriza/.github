Maps to the three threads:
1. Process / ingestion — the pre-deploy Slack card IS the lightweight ping. What's going out + when + a customer-ready one-liner, before the button is pushed.
2. Scheduling & logistics — urgency chip (green/yellow/red) tells you at a glance whether a release can wait for a calmer window. Shared calendar of demos/webinars is the open piece (not built yet — visibility, not approval).
3. Release strategy — post-deploy changelog (what shipped) + pre-deploy preflight (what's coming). Both land in #product-release-notes. No dashboard to remember to check; the ping comes to you.
